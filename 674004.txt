During a press conference in Seoul , South Korea in August 2010 , Island Def Jam executive Matt Voss announced that the album would be out on November 2 , 2010 , and would include six new songs as well as a remix of her classic hit " All I Want for Christmas Is You " . The album , titled Merry Christmas II You , was released alongside an accompanying DVD , and was sent to retailers on November 2 , 2010 .
