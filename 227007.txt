In response to Golden Harvest 's withdrawal from the film industry in 2003 , Chan started his own film production company , JCE Movies Limited ( Jackie Chan Emperor Movies Limited ) in association with Emperor Multimedia Group ( EMG ) . His films have since featured an increasing number of dramatic scenes while continuing to succeed at the box office ; examples include New Police Story ( 2004 ) , The Myth ( 2005 ) and the hit film Rob - B - Hood ( 2006 ) . Chan 's next release was the third instalment in the Rush Hour series : Rush Hour 3 in August 2007 .
