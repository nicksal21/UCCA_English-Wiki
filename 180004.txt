Gyllenhaal has appeared in an eclectic range of films including Sherrybaby ( 2006 ) for which she was nominated for a Golden Globe ; the romantic comedy , Trust The Man ( 2006 ) ; and numerous big - budget films such as World Trade Center ( 2006 ) and The Dark Knight ( 2008 ) . She next starred in the musical - drama , Crazy Heart ( 2009 ) , for which she was nominated for an Academy Award for Best Supporting Actress .
