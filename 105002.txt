She appeared in five of her brother 's student films , made while he attended the USC School of Cinema - Television , as well as in several music videos , namely Lenny Kravitz 's " Stand by My Woman " ( 1991 ) , Antonello Venditti 's " Alta Marea " ( 1991 ) , The Lemonheads 's " It 's About Time " ( 1993 ) , and Meat Loaf 's " Rock and Roll Dreams Come Through " ( 1993 ) . She began to learn from her father , as she noticed his method of observing people to become like them .
