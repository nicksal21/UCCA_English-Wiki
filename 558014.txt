Moore recalled , " During the instrumental parts he would back off from the mike and be playing and shaking , and the crowd would just go wild " . Black , a natural showman , whooped and rode his bass , hitting double licks that Presley would later remember as " really a wild sound , like a jungle drum or something " . Soon after , Moore and Black quit their old band to play with Presley regularly , and DJ and promoter Bob Neal became the trio 's manager .
