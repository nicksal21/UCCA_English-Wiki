The album earned ten Grammy Award nominations in 2006 - 07 : eight in 2006 for the original release ( the most received by Carey in a single year ) , and two in 2007 for the Ultra Platinum Edition ( from which " Do n't Forget About Us " became her seventeenth # 1 hit ) . In 2006 Carey won Best Contemporary R & B Album for The Emancipation of Mimi , as well as Best Female R & B Vocal Performance and Best R & B Song for " We Belong Together " . The Emancipation of Mimi was the best - selling album in the United States in 2005 , with nearly five million units sold .
