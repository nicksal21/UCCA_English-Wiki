Peaking at number six in the UK , the album yielded the hits " Day - In , Day - Out " ( his 60th single ) , " Time Will Crawl " , and " Never Let Me Down " . Bowie later described it as his " nadir " , calling it " an awful album " . Supporting Never Let Me Down , and preceded by nine promotional press shows , the 86 - concert Glass Spider Tour commenced on 30 May .
