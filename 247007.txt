Hawke regarded Tape as his " first adult performance " , a performance noted by Roger Ebert for its " physical and verbal acting mastery " . Hawke 's next role , and one for which he received substantial critical acclaim , came in Training Day ( 2001 ) . Hawke played rookie cop Jake Hoyt , alongside Denzel Washington , as one of a pair of narcotics detectives from the Los Angeles Police Department spending 24 hours in the gang neighborhoods of South Los Angeles .
