Her first appearance came at the age of two - and - a - half when she joined her two older sisters , Mary Jane " Suzy / Suzanne " Gumm ( 1915 - 1964 ) and Dorothy Virginia " Jimmie " Gumm ( 1917 - 1977 ) , on the stage of her father 's movie theater during a Christmas show and sang a chorus of " Jingle Bells " . Accompanied by their mother on piano , The Gumm Sisters performed there for the next few years .
