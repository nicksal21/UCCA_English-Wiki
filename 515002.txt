He particularly strove to emulate the British musical theatre singer - songwriter and actor Anthony Newley , whose vocal style he frequently adopted , and made prominent use of for his 1967 debut release , David Bowie ( to the disgust of Newley himself , who destroyed the copy he received from Bowie 's publisher ) . Bowie 's music hall fascination continued to surface sporadically alongside such diverse styles as hard rock and heavy metal , soul , psychedelic folk and pop .
