The Fort Worth Star - Telegram reviewer wrote that " Hawke , building on the sympathetic - but - edgy presence that has served him well since his kid - actor days , is most impressive " . 1998 saw Hawke appearing in Great Expectations , a contemporary film adaptation of the Charles Dickens novel , and collaborating for a second time with director Richard Linklater in The Newton Boys , based on the true story of the Newton Gang , a family of bank robbers from Uvalde , Texas .
