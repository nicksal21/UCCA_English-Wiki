In 1985 , a portrait of her was included in a series of United Kingdom postage stamps , along with Sir Alfred Hitchcock , Sir Charlie Chaplin , Peter Sellers and David Niven to commemorate " British Film Year " . In April 2013 she was again included in a series of U.K. postage stamps , this time celebrating the 100th anniversary of her birth , achieving the rare accolade of a non - royal family member to appear on a British postage stamp on more than one occasion .
