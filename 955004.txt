Hollywood was in the midst of a widely publicised search to find an actress to portray Scarlett O'Hara in David O. Selznick 's production of Gone with the Wind ( 1939 ) . Leigh 's American theatrical agent was the London representative of the Myron Selznick Agency ( Myron was David 's brother ) . In February 1938 , Leigh asked that she be allowed to play Scarlett O'Hara .
