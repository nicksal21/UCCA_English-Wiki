It included a number of cover songs , among them the 1966 Beach Boys hit " God Only Knows " . The album bore the transatlantic top ten hit " Blue Jean " , itself the inspiration for a short film that won Bowie a Grammy Award for Best Short Form Music Video , " Jazzin ' for Blue Jean " . Bowie performed at Wembley in 1985 for Live Aid , a multi - venue benefit concert for Ethiopian famine relief .
