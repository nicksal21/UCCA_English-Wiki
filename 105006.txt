Following a supporting role in the independent film Without Evidence ( 1995 ) , Jolie starred as Kate " Acid Burn " Libby in her first Hollywood picture , Hackers ( 1995 ) . The New York Times wrote , " Kate ( Angelina Jolie ) stands out . That 's because she scowls even more sourly than [ her co - stars ] and is that rare female hacker who sits intently at her keyboard in a see - through top . Despite her sullen posturing , which is all this role requires , Ms. Jolie has the sweetly cherubic looks of her father , Jon Voight . "
