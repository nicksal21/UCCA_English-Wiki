With six number one albums , Carey is tied with Britney Spears and Janet Jackson in the United States for the third most number one albums for a female artist , behind Madonna with seven and Barbra Streisand 's nine chart toppers . In 2008 , Billboard magazine ranked her at number six on the " Billboard Hot 100 All - Time Top Artists " , making Carey the second most successful female artist in the history of the Billboard Hot 100 chart .
