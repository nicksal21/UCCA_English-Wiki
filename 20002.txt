Pitt has stated that his family 's ancestry consists of " probably ... Irish - Scots - Germans who settled in the area ... I know we have some Seminole , and some Cherokee Indian , in us " . Pitt has described Springfield as " Mark Twain country , Jesse James country " , having grown up with " a lot of hills , a lot of lakes " . Pitt attended Kickapoo High School , where he was a member of the golf , tennis , wrestling and swimming teams .
