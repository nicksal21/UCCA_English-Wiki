During the tour Bowie 's observation of two seminal American proto - punk artists led him to develop a concept that eventually found form in the Ziggy Stardust character : a melding of the persona of Iggy Pop with the music of Lou Reed , producing " the ultimate pop idol " . A girlfriend recalled his " scrawling notes on a cocktail napkin about a crazy rock star named Iggy or Ziggy " , and on his return to England he declared his intention to create a character " who looks like he 's landed from Mars " .
