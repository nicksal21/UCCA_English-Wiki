In May 1969 , Dylan appeared on the first episode of Johnny Cash 's new television show , duetting with Cash on " Girl from the North Country " , " I Threw It All Away " , and " Living the Blues " . Dylan next traveled to England to top the bill at the Isle of Wight rock festival on August 31 , 1969 , after rejecting overtures to appear at the Woodstock Festival far closer to his home .
