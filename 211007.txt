Hoffman adopted Karina ( b . 1966 ) , Byrne 's child from a previous marriage , and with Byrne had daughter Jenna ( born October 15 , 1970 ) . In 1970 , Hoffman and Byrne were living in Greenwich Village in a building next door to the townhouse destroyed by members of the Weathermen when they detonated a bomb in the building 's basement , killing three people .
