Mike Bloomfield was unwilling to leave the Butterfield Band , so Dylan mixed Al Kooper and Harvey Brooks from his studio crew with Robbie Robertson and Levon Helm , best known at the time for being part of Ronnie Hawkins 's backing band The Hawks ( later to become The Band ) . On August 28 at Forest Hills Tennis Stadium , the group was heckled by an audience still annoyed by Dylan 's electric sound .
