The tour marked the first time McCartney performed Beatles songs live with Wings , with five in the two - hour set list : " I 've Just Seen a Face " , " Yesterday " , " Blackbird " , " Lady Madonna " and " The Long and Winding Road " . Following the second European leg of the tour and extensive rehearsals in London , the group undertook an ambitious US arena tour that yielded the US number - one live triple LP Wings over America .
