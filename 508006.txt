Making prominent use of electronic instruments , the album , which reunited Bowie with Let 's Dance producer Nile Rodgers , confirmed Bowie 's return to popularity , hitting the number one spot on the UK charts and spawning three top 40 hits , including the top 10 song " Jump They Say " . Bowie explored new directions on The Buddha of Suburbia ( 1993 ) , a soundtrack album of incidental music composed for the TV series adaptation of Hanif Kureishi 's novel .
