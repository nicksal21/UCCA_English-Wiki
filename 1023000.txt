Time described Jackson 's influence at that point as " Star of records , radio , rock video . A one - man rescue team for the music business . A songwriter who sets the beat for a decade . A dancer with the fanciest feet on the street . A singer who cuts across all boundaries of taste and style and color too " . The New York Times wrote that , " in the world of pop music , there is Michael Jackson and there is everybody else " . On March 25 , 1983 , Michael Jackson reunited with his brothers for a legendary live performance which was taped for a Motown 25 : Yesterday , Today , Forever television special .
