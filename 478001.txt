Johnson shares the single - game playoff record for assists ( 24 ) , holds the Finals record for assists in a game ( 21 ) , and has the most playoff assists ( 2,346 ) . He holds the All - Star Game single - game record for assists ( 22 ) , and the All - Star Game record for career assists ( 127 ) . Johnson introduced a fast - paced style of basketball called " Showtime " , described as a mix of " no - look passes off the fastbreak , pin - point alley - oops from halfcourt , spinning feeds and overhand bullets under the basket through triple teams .
