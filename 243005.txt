Other films have included the science fiction feature Gattaca ( 1997 ) , the title role in Michael Almereyda 's Hamlet ( 2000 ) , the action thriller Assault on Precinct 13 ( 2005 ) , and the crime drama Before the Devil Knows You 're Dead ( 2007 ) . Hawke has appeared in many theater productions including The Seagull , Henry IV , Hurlyburly , The Cherry Orchard , The Winter 's Tale and The Coast of Utopia , for which he earned a Tony Award nomination .
