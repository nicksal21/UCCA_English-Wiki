In 1980 , Jackson won three awards at the American Music Awards for his solo efforts : Favorite Soul / R & B Album , Favorite Soul / R & B Male Artist , and Favorite Soul / R & B Single for " Do n't Stop ' Til You Get Enough " . That year , he also won Billboard Year - End for Top Black Artist and Top Black Album and a Grammy Award for Best Male R & B Vocal Performance , also for " Do n't Stop ' Til You Get Enough " . Jackson again won at the American Music Awards in 1981 for Favorite Soul / R & B Album and Favorite Soul / R & B Male Artist .
