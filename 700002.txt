In 1967 he began recording music with the Hawks at his home and in the basement of the Hawks ' nearby house , called " Big Pink " . These songs , initially compiled as demos for other artists to record , provided hit singles for Julie Driscoll ( " This Wheel 's on Fire " ) , The Byrds ( " You Ai n't Goin ' Nowhere " , " Nothing Was Delivered " ) , and Manfred Mann ( " Mighty Quinn " ) . Columbia released selections from them in 1975 as The Basement Tapes .
